ABOUT ECOSORT

SORT SMARTER
LIVE GREENER

EcoSort isn’t just another recycling app. We want to make it effortless for everyone to dispose of waste correctly - no more confusion about bins, colors or local rules.

With EcoSort you will spend less time worrying about “Where does this go?” and more time feeling good about helping the planet. We will provide the information and tools - you do the sorting.

If you have feedback, feature requests or questions, reach out anytime at hello@ecosort.app.
